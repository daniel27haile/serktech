The Story

Why We Started Serk Technologies

Too many small businesses and founders were spending money on websites that looked cheap, loaded slowly, or simply didn't convert. And too many vendors were delivering code that worked in a demo but fell apart in production.

We built Serk Technologies around one idea: that good software should be accessible to any business, not just enterprise companies with large engineering teams. Every client we work with gets the same attention to detail, the same quality of code, and the same honest communication — regardless of project size.

Our background is in building scalable web applications: Angular and React frontends, Node.js backends, MongoDB databases, and AWS cloud infrastructure. Our real specialization is turning a business requirement into a working, reliable product — and delivering it on time.
